Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Welcome to Olik Lab ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our applications and services available on the Google Play Store and other platforms.

By using our applications, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the features of our applications.

1.1. Personal Information

  • Account Data: For apps requiring login, we may collect your email address and name (via Google Sign-In or email Auth) to create and secure your account.
  • User Content: Data you explicitly enter into the app (e.g., preferences, tracking logs, settings) is stored to provide the service.

1.2. Device & Usage Information

We may collect limited non-personal information about your device to ensure app stability and performance:

  • Device model and operating system version.
  • Crash logs and performance metrics (anonymized).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our applications.
  • To sync your data securely across your devices (where cloud sync is applicable).
  • To improve user experience and fix technical issues.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to facilitate our Service:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure cloud storage, authentication, and database services.
  • Google AdMob: Some of our free applications may display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to show non-personalized or personalized ads based on your consent settings.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

  • You can delete your data directly within our applications using the provided delete functions.
  • You can request full account deletion by contacting us. Upon deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
